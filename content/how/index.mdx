# 7 Missions. Your Own AI Agent.

20 minutes per mission. No server experience needed. Complete all 7 and reach **Commander rank**.

Every mission ends with something real you can show someone — not a tutorial exercise, but an actual AI agent doing something useful on your behalf.

---

## What you're building

By Mission 7, you will have:

- A personal AI agent running on your own machine, under your control
- A custom name, personality, and set of values it carries into every conversation
- Mobile access via Telegram — it texts you, you text back
- A morning briefing sent to your phone every day without you asking
- Web research and file tools fully operational
- A team of specialized sub-agents working in parallel for you
- A Commander rank certificate
